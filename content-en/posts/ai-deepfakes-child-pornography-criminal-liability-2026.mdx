### Quick Takeaways

- Creation and dissemination of sexual deepfakes with **real faces of minors**: high probability of fitting within **child sexual abuse material** (Arts. 189 et seq. Spanish Criminal Code).
- The face is **personal data**: processing without legal basis violates **GDPR/LOPDGDD**; the administrative route **does not exclude** criminal or civil liability.
- Platforms and administrators: duties of **moderation**, **detection**, **expeditious removal**, and **evidence preservation**.

### What Conduct Generates Liability

- **Creating** sexually explicit synthetic images with identification of minors.
- **Disseminating** (messaging, social media, hosting) or **storing** for distribution.
- **Facilitating** technical means for creation or propagation.

### Criminal Offenses and Accumulation with GDPR

- **Child sexual abuse material**, **disclosure of secrets**, and, where applicable, **defamation/slander** with massive dissemination.
- **AEPD sanctions** are compatible with **criminal liability** and **civil damage claims**.
