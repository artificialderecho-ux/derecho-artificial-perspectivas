## 1. Introduction: The Paradigm Shift in Automated Recruiting

> **Related Analysis: Case Eeoc V Itutorgroup**: [caso eeoc v itutorgroup](/jurisprudencia/caso-eeoc-v-itutorgroup)

From the analytical perspective of derechoartificial.com, we have maintained that Artificial Intelligence (AI) is not an aseptic tool, but an *economic machina* that acts as a prism of pre-existing social structures.

The case **EEOC v. iTutorGroup** must not be read as an isolated incident, but as the **foundational milestone of the algorithmic liability frontier**. We are witnessing the birth of a new era of employment litigation where technical opacity no longer serves as refuge from civil rights scrutiny.
