# AI in Legal Practice: Strategic Integration, Ethics, and Competitiveness

An in-depth analysis of AI integration into legal practice. This article explores how AI is not a passing trend, but a strategic imperative that redefines competitiveness and ethics in modern law firms.

The symbiosis between the attorney and the algorithm requires evolution in legal education. In this analysis, Ricardo Scarpa examines the deontological boundaries and efficiency opportunities presented by this technology.

### Key Points:

* **Human Supervision:** Critical judgment as a safeguard against AI hallucinations.
* **Competitiveness:** How AI optimizes management in small and medium-sized firms.
* **Professional Secrecy:** Technical challenges in handling confidential data.

This text establishes the foundation for understanding the attorney's role in an automated legal market.
